## üßí Kid-Friendly Design Principles

### Simple Rules, Deep Strategy
- All mechanics have **clear visual indicators**
- **No complex math** - just "more damage" or "less damage"
- **Fun animations** for every special event
- **Emoji-based communication** for easy understanding

### Positive Reinforcement
- **Lots of visual feedback** for good plays
- **Celebration moments** for achievements
- **No punishing mechanics** - even status conditions are temporary
- **Lucky moments** add surprise and excitement

### Progressive Complexity
- **Base game** stays the same for beginners
- **Advanced features** activate naturally as players play more
- **Optional depth** - kids can ignore complex mechanics and still have fun
